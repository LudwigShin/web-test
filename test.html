The Sea and The Butterfly
-Girim Kim

As no-one has taught him how deep the sea is,
The white Butterfly is quite fearless.

Be it a field of as-yet blue radishes? he descends,
And returns, spent as a princess,
Young wings soaked with the waves.

The azure moon is cold on the length of the Butterfly;
He is sad, for the sea in springtime brings no flowers.
